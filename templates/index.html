About Onam Festival

Kerala's Most Celebrated Festival

Onam is a harvest festival celebrated in Kerala with great pomp and pageantry. It marks the homecoming of the legendary King Mahabali and is celebrated across Kerala, irrespective of caste, creed, or religion.

The festival spans 10 days and is filled with traditional games, dance forms like Kathakali and Mohiniyattam, elaborate feasts, and the creation of beautiful flower arrangements called Pookalam.

Our college celebrates Onam with grandeur, bringing students together to experience the richness of Kerala's cultural heritage.
